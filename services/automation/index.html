Electrical Services Automation

At Kansas Electric, we specialize in providing innovative automation solutions tailored to your facility’s unique needs. From design to integration, our services are focused on enhancing your efficiency and productivity while minimizing disruptions to your operations.

Our expertise includes:

  • Expertise with leading platforms, including:

    • Allen Bradley (PLC 5 to recent models)
    • Siemens
    • GE
    • Productivity

  • Advanced monitoring and control solutions with:

    • Ignition
    • Aveva/Wonderware
    • FactoryTalk View ME/SE
    • Optix
    • PlantPAX
    • GE iFIX
    • Custom design and construction of control panels
    • Compliance with UL safety standards (UL508A and UL698A)

  • Integration and support for:

    • Allen Bradley
    • ABB
    • Schneider

  • Upgrading obsolete PLCs and control panels to improve performance and reliability.

Why choose Kansas Electric?

Automation is the backbone of efficiency in today’s facilities. Let Kansas Electric bring your operations to the cutting edge.

  • Seamless Integration Connecting modern systems to legacy infrastructure with precision.

  • Minimal Downtime We work during planned downtimes or weekends to ensure no interruptions to your production.

  • Tailored Solutions Customized automation designed to meet your unique operational challenges.
