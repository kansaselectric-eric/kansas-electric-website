Electrical Services Industrial

Our team of skilled industrial electricians is dedicated to providing advanced solutions for your facility's electrical requirements. We are eager to arrange a meeting with you to assess how our expertise in industrial electrical systems can contribute to improving the efficiency and safety of your operations. You can trust us to deliver customized solutions that align with your industrial electrical needs.

  • We know the transmission of electrical power is vital to keep your facility running. We offer ongoing maintenance and upgrades to ensure a reliable power distribution infrastructure.

    • Motor Controls Motor controls utilize devices such as starters, contactors, and variable frequency drives. Integrating these electrical components effectively controls motor operation to meet your needs. The result is improved energy efficiency and enhanced system reliability across diverse applications.
    • Thermal Imaging Study We will help you predetermine potential electrical faults by using state-of-the-art infrared cameras to detect and visualize thermal patterns on your electrical systems. The resulting infrared images, or thermograms, show variations in temperature that may indicate issues such as:
      • Loose connections
      • Insulation deficiencies
      • Equipment degradation
      We will provide you with an organized report that you can use as a baseline reference. Many of our customers do this study on an annual basis.
    • Transformer Oil Sample Testing We will collect and analyze oil samples from your transformers to determine their health and efficiency. This preventative maintenance service has been most effective in predicting potential transformer catastrophic failures. Early detection will extend the lifespan of your transformers and prevent costly downtime.
    • Breaker Exercising Another preventative maintenance service we offer is breaker exercising. This service includes opening and closing circuit breakers under controlled conditions to verify their performance and reliability. Breaker exercising helps identify issues such as:
      • Corrosion
      • Contamination
      • Mechanical deterioration that can affect the breaker's ability to offer reliable overcurrent protection.
    • Switchgear Cleaning We offer routine Switchgear cleaning. This involves removing dirt and contaminants from electrical switchgear. We are experienced at cleaning your materials using proper PPE to prevent arc faults. Regular cleaning is essential for the reliability and safety of electrical systems.
    • Engineered Arc Flash Study We provide arc flash studies to comply with safety and insurance standards. This is a systematic analysis of your electrical system to identify the risks associated with arc flash incidents. Key steps:
      • Data collection,
      • Arc flash hazard analysis in conjunction with an electrical engineer,
      • Applying arc flash labeling to your electrical equipment, detailing protective measures (proper PPE needed)
      The study aims to enhance worker safety, comply with OSHA regulations, and minimize the impact of arc flashes on personnel.
    • Power Quality Our skilled technicians will monitor your facility for power quality. This service will improve the quality of your electrical power. We utilize cutting-edge power quality monitors to analyze your electrical systems in order to identify potential problems that could cause damage to delicate electronic devices.

  • We are passionate about energy conservation. Possibly the easiest way to reduce power consumption is to upgrade your lighting systems. We offer ROI analysis for energy efficiency and improved lumens for you to consider. Transitioning to the latest LED technology and integrating smart solutions will improve efficiency.

  • We specialize in moving electrical equipment safely and efficiently within facilities no matter how large or small. Our services include planning, disconnecting, relocating, and reconnecting panels, machinery, and systems. We ensure safety compliance, minimize downtime, and execute relocations with precision.

  • We are certified to design and build UL listed electrical panels of any size. Our technicians will help design and build panels according to your specifications.

  • We design and install security systems that will provide you with peace of mind. We will integrate a security system that alerts and records unwonted events at your place of business.

  • Our technicians are capable of servicing and installing medium voltage systems up to 15,000 volts. We are equipped to handle emergencies with our stock Inventory. Our services include VLF testing, terminations on medium voltage switchgear and transformers.
