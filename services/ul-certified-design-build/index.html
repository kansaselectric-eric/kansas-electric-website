Custom UL-Certified Control Panels

Built, Tested & Shipped Nationwide

Precision-built UL 508A & UL 698A panels for industrial applications across the U.S.

Whether you need a custom design or a build-to-spec solution, our state-of-the-art UL-certified panel shop delivers high-quality, compliant control panels — ready to ship wherever you need them.

  • Custom Design & Assembly Whether you need a fully custom-designed control panel or a build-to-spec solution, our team ensures every panel meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and performance. From concept to final assembly, we provide expert engineering and precision craftsmanship.
  • Nationwide Shipping & Support No matter where your project is located, we make it easy to get the control panels you need. We handle secure packaging, logistics, and delivery to ensure your UL-certified panels arrive ready for seamless installation. Need support? Our team is here to assist you every step of the way.

UL-Certified Panel Build Shop

Our UL-certified panel build shop is equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by highly skilled professionals. This certification underscores our commitment to producing safe, high-quality control panels that meet stringent industry standards. From concept to completion, we ensure precision and quality in every build, providing peace of mind to our clients.

Expertise in UL 508A and UL 698A Standards

We specialize in designing and building control panels that comply with UL 508A and UL 698A certifications:

  • UL 508A

    This certification ensures our panels meet the highest standards for industrial control equipment, offering optimal performance and safety in a variety of industrial applications.

  • UL 698A

    Our expertise in this certification highlights our ability to design and construct panels suitable for hazardous locations, ensuring compliance with rigorous safety requirements.
