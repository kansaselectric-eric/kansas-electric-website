Careers

Why work at Kansas Electric?

  • Competitive Compensation We offer industry-leading pay and benefits.

  • Merit-Based Promotions Advance in your career based on your performance and skills.

  • Travel Opportunities Enjoy per diem, lodging, and mileage reimbursement for qualified positions.

  • Career Growth & Training Participate in in-house leadership development, advanced certifications, and hands-on field experience.

  • Diverse & Exciting Projects Work on industrial automation, data centers, renewable energy, and more.

  • Strong Safety Culture Your well-being is our priority, with best-in-class training and equipment.

  • Apprenticeship Program Our accredited program allows you to earn while you learn, providing hands-on experience under the guidance of licensed journeymen.

Join a Team That Powers the Future

At Kansas Electric, we don't just build electrical systems—we build careers. If you're looking to take control of your career, we offer higher pay, faster promotions, and more opportunities to work on high-impact industrial and commercial projects.

  • Electrical Foreman (Traveling & Local)

    $3,000 Sign-On Bonus

    • Lead crews on large-scale industrial and commercial projects.
    • Oversee jobsite safety, timelines, and material management.
    • Journeyman license required, leadership experience preferred.
    • Advance based on skill, not seniority.

  • Journeyman Electrician (Traveling & Local)

    $3,000 Sign-On Bonus

    • Experience in industrial and commercial electrical work required.
    • Ability to read and interpret blueprints, schematics, and electrical drawings.
    • Strong troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
    • Earn competitive pay with clear paths for career advancement.
